Book your accommodation ASAP! It’s a holiday weekend, so hotel rooms will go fast. We chose not to have a room block, but have included some suggestions below.

Flight

Chicago O’Hare (ORD) is the most convenient airport. The Blue Line train from O’Hare will cost $5 and take you right into the city. Just follow signs for “Train to City,” tap to pay, and hop on. You literally cannot get on the wrong one. Taxi is ~30 min and ~$50. Call us if you’re confused!

Where to Stay

The Robey: We’ll be here — center of Wicker Park, steps from the Damen Blue Line stop.

Hyatt Place Wicker Park: Across from Hollywood Grill (24 hours, huge vibes).

Wicker Park Inn: Scurto family tested, approved.

Ray’s Bucktown B&B: Real Chicago energy.

Airbnb: Search near the Blue Line — Wicker Park, Bucktown, Humboldt Park, West Town, Ukrainian Village, Logan Square.
